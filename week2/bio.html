Critique of "panic.com"

The website panic.com is intended for commercial use. The site uses aesthetically pleasing pictures as links to its various products. As a consumer I appreciate the fact that the prices for the products the site is selling are clearly stated. The videos that accompany the product description pages are extremely useful. After watching for only a few moments I can easily tell the intended use of each product. Furthermore, the ability to submit item specific questions to the website is a very user friendly addition.
In my opinion the site could be improved with just a few changes. First, I would include audio with the videos. The audio would better help prospective customers understand the products. Second, if not audio I would like to see a short and concise written product description. Lastly, I would have liked to have seen perhaps bullet points describing why the panic.com products are better than the competition. Overall, panic.com was a great site but could still be improved to better serve its audience.
