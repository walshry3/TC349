Biography of Ryan Walsh

Ryan Walsh is a senior at Michigan State University. He is currently studying Applied Engineering Sciences with a concentration in Supply Chain Logistics. Drawn to the field of study by its complexity, Ryan loves to solve problems. While he has enjoyed increasing his analytical skills during his most recent internship at Jackson National Life Insurance, he has also found the professional development of interpersonal communication to be a growing asset in the work place. Ryan looks forward to building on his past experiences as he enters the Industrial Engineering masters program next fall at Western Michigan University.
Living by his fraternal motto to "strive for the highest", Ryan loves a good challenge. One of his greatest challenges and greatest accomplishments was his successful completion of four years in the Spartan Marching Band. As a baritonist and trombonist he enjoys to practice and listen to music. In his additional spare time, Ryan follows up on local Detroit sports teams and enjoys participating himself. As a former hockey player Ryan believes that competition is one of his greatest motivators.
